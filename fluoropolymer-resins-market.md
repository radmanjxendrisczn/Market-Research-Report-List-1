<p><p>The fluoropolymer resins market is categorized into several types based on their chemical properties and applications. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is known for its high-temperature resistance and non-stick properties. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) offers excellent chemical resistance and is used in piping and coatings. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) combines the properties of PTFE and FEP, making it versatile for high-performance applications. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) is valued for its transparency and processability. Other types include specialized fluoropolymers catering to niche applications.</p></p>
