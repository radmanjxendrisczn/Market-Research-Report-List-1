<p><strong>The PCB Soldering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wave Soldering Oven</li><li>Reflow Oven</li><li>Selective Oven</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB soldering system market comprises three main types: wave soldering ovens, reflow ovens, and selective soldering systems. Wave soldering ovens are used for through-hole components, creating a wave of molten solder to bond joints. Reflow ovens, essential for surface-mounted devices, heat solder paste to melt and form connections after placement. Selective soldering systems offer precision by directing solder only to specific areas, ideal for mixed technology boards. Each type addresses different assembly needs in electronics manufacturing.</p></p>
