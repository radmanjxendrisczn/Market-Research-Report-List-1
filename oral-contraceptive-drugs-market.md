<p><p>The oral contraceptive drugs market is categorized into three main types: long-acting contraceptives, short-acting contraceptives, and emergency contraceptives. Long-acting contraceptives, such as implants and injections, provide extended pregnancy prevention with fewer administrations. Short-acting contraceptives include daily pills that require consistent use for effectiveness. Emergency contraceptives are intended for use after unprotected intercourse to prevent pregnancy. Each type serves specific needs, contributing to diverse options for reproductive health management and family planning.</p></p>
