<p><strong>The Iron Ore Pellets Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Heat Exchange</li><li>Indirect Heat Exchange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Iron Ore Pellets Dryer market is segmented into direct and indirect heat exchange types. Direct heat exchange involves directly heating the iron ore pellets with hot air or gas, leading to efficient moisture removal but potential risk of thermal degradation. In contrast, indirect heat exchange uses heated surfaces to transfer heat to the pellets without direct contact, ensuring gentler drying and reduced contamination risk. Both methods aim to enhance pellet quality and energy efficiency in the iron ore processing industry.</p></p>
