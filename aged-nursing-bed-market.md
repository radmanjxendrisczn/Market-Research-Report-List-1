<p><p>The aged nursing bed market is categorized into two main types: automatic and manual. Automatic beds provide advanced features such as adjustable positions, powered movement, and often include additional functionalities like massage options and safety alarms, catering to users with limited mobility. They enhance comfort and ease of use for caregivers. In contrast, manual beds are simpler and rely on hand-cranks for adjustments. They are typically more cost-effective and suitable for settings where advanced technology is not necessary. Both types serve essential roles in elderly care.</p></p>
