<p><p>The photosensitive solder resist ink market is categorized into solvent-based and non-solvent-based types. Solvent-based inks contain organic solvents, allowing for easy application and quick drying but often come with environmental regulations. Non-solvent-based inks, on the other hand, utilize water or eco-friendly alternatives, promoting safer handling and reduced VOC emissions. Both types are essential for creating solder masks in printed circuit boards, with choices influenced by performance requirements, environmental concerns, and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
