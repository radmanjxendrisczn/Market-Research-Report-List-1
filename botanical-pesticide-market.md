<p><p>The botanical pesticide market includes several distinct types, such as phytotoxins, phytogenous insect hormones, and others. Phytotoxins are naturally derived toxins that target specific pests without harming beneficial organisms. Phytogenous insect hormones are plant-derived substances that regulate insect growth and behavior, effectively disrupting pest life cycles. Other botanical pesticides encompass a range of natural products, including essential oils and plant extracts, which offer pest control through various mechanisms. Together, these types promote sustainable agricultural practices by minimizing chemical residues.</p></p>
