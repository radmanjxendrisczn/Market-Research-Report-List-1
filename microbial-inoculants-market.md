<p><p>The microbial inoculants market is categorized into three main types: bacterial, fungal, and composite inoculants. Bacterial inoculants consist of beneficial bacteria that enhance soil fertility and crop health. Fungal inoculants include mycorrhizal fungi that improve nutrient uptake and soil structure. Composite inoculants combine both bacteria and fungi to provide synergistic benefits, promoting plant growth and resilience. These inoculants play a crucial role in sustainable agriculture by improving soil health, increasing crop yields, and reducing reliance on chemical fertilizers.</p></p>
