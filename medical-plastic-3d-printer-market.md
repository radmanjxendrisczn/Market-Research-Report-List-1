<p><p>The medical plastic 3D printer market encompasses various technologies, including Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), Fused Granular Fabrication (FGF), and Laser Metal Fusion (LMF). FDM uses thermoplastic materials to create detailed structures layer by layer, making it ideal for prototypes and patient-specific devices. FGF employs granules instead of filaments, allowing for a broader range of materials and faster production. LMF focuses on metal parts, suitable for tools and implants. Other technologies may include vat polymerization and binder jetting, expanding application possibilities.</p></p>
